Code Geass is a Japanese anime series set in an alternate universe where the world is divided into three superpowers: the Holy Britannian Empire, the Chinese Federation, and the European Union. The story follows the journey of Lelouch vi Britannia, a young exiled prince of the Holy Britannian Empire, who gains the power to control anyone he desires. But only once per person. The show is full of political intrigue, action, and suspense, as Lelouch navigates the complex world of power struggles and war. He must make difficult choices and alliances as he fights for his cause, while trying to keep his identity hidden from those who would oppose him.

Favourite Episode:

ZERO

Without giving away any spoilers, the last episode of Season 1 of Code Geass is full of twists and turns that will leave you wanting more. A well-crafted master piece in my opinion. It sets up the stage for an even more intense and thrilling second season and boasts one of the best endings to an anime series I have watched.
